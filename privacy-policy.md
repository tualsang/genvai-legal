## PRIVACY POLICY FOR GENVAI

Last updated: February 3, 2026

Genvai ("we", "our", "us") operates the 
Genvai mobile application ("the App").

WHAT INFORMATION WE COLLECT

We collect the following information:
- Member name and policy number (provided by ZIUSA)
- State/region assignment (assigned by ZIUSA)
- Payment screenshot images (uploaded by members)
- Device push notification token (for sending status updates)
- Email address (for admin accounts only)

HOW WE COLLECT IT

- Member information is entered by organization administrators
- Payment screenshots are voluntarily uploaded by members
- Push notification tokens are collected when you allow notifications
- We do not collect information automatically or use tracking/analytics

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

- To display your membership payment status
- To allow administrators to verify your payments
- To send you push notifications about your payment status
- To maintain records of payment submissions

WHO CAN SEE YOUR INFORMATION

- Organization administrators assigned to your region can view your 
  submissions and payment status
- Super administrators can view all member information
- We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties
- We do not use your data for advertising

WHERE YOUR DATA IS STORED

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (supabase.com) servers. 
Payment screenshot images are stored in Supabase Storage with managed 
access controls.

DATA RETENTION

- Member records are retained as long as your membership is active
- Payment screenshots are retained for [1 MONTH / DURATION]
- You may request deletion of your data at any time

YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:
- Request a copy of your personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt out of push notifications via device settings

HOW TO CONTACT US

For questions about this privacy policy or to exercise your rights:
Email: tualsang@outlook.com

CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted 
within the app or on our website.
